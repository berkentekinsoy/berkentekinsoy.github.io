Designing

First, we create our project and start to design. This is almost the most important part because if a game looks great, then people will want to play it even if they don't know the content. For designing, we use Aseprite and Photoshop.

Programming and Animation

The second part is programming and animation. We use Unity Engine and C# to program our game. A clean code and nice mechanics bring a good feedback. At this part, we also animate our characters and design levels.

Publishing

The last part is publishing. We publish our games on Google Play but soon we may also use ITCH.IO.
